Our Story

Bluestock Fintech is a leading financial technology company dedicated to democratizing access to IPO investments and market intelligence.

Founded in 2020, we recognized the challenges individual investors face when trying to access and understand IPO opportunities. Our platform was built to bridge this gap, providing comprehensive IPO tracking, analysis, and application services.

Today, we serve thousands of investors across India, helping them make informed investment decisions and participate in the country's growing capital markets.

Our Mission

To democratize access to IPO investments by providing transparent, comprehensive, and user-friendly tools that empower individual investors to make informed decisions.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted platform for IPO tracking and investment, making capital markets accessible to every Indian investor.
